by Gregory Thom

Shang-Chi, the master of unarmed weaponry-based Kung Fu, is forced to confront his past after drawn into the Ten Rings organization - the same terror group who's behind Tony Stark's kidnapping in Iron Man. In the comics, Shang-Chi is a master of kung-fu, similarly growing up as the heir to the 10 Rings. His father, Fu Manchu, is regarded as a problematic portrayal of xenophobic tropes, so the MCU is adjusting Shang-Chi's origins so that the longtime Iron Man villain, the Mandarin, is his father instead. Even though he often works alone, Shang-Chi goes on to fight alongside Captain America, Daredevil, and the X-Men.
